View on Google Maps

The Dineen Coffee Co. building is on the ground floor of the famous Dineen Building. We're located at the corner of Yonge Street and Temperance Street, in downtown Toronto. Our store is a comfortable 10 minute walk from Union Station, and we're just steps away from both the Queen and King subway stations.

The ambiance of our store is as important to us as the coffee we serve. We have comfortable indoor and outdoor seating. We serve baked goods and sandwiches in addition to our signature coffees and parfaits. Whether it's to grab a quick caffeine fix, or to relax during an extended coffee break, our helpful and attentive staff is here to serve you efficiently, and with care.

Stop by our store during your next visit to downtown Toronto, and experience the coffee and the ambiance for yourself!
